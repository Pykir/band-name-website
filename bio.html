Bio

Mid-Air Thief started his career in 2012, releasing 2 mini albums under the stage name of Hyoo (휴). His distinctive sound had already begun to develop, and in 2015 the project's name was changed to Gongjoong Doduk (공중도덕, Public Morality). Under this alias, his first full album, also titled Gongjoong Doduk, was released. It was nominated for best Rock Modern Rock Album at the 2016 Korean Music Awards. 2016 was also the year that the name became Mid-Air Thief (공중도둑). His 2018 album, Crumbling, propelled the project to critical acclaim, gaining major attention across online communities and winning Best Dance & Electronic Album at the 2018 Korean Music Awards. Since then, he has released 2 more albums under new monikers: Flood Format under the name Bird's Eye Batang (새눈바탕) and Flash under the name Bird's Eye Thief (새눈도둑).
